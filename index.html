Spider-Man

About Spider-Man

Spider-Man, The Amazing Spider-Man, The Incredible Spider-Man, and other adjectives that former Daily Bugle CEO, J. Jonah. Jameson likes to use. These are only some of many ways to describe Spider-Man, the web-headed *Pun intended* hero of Manhattan. Some say he's a menance, a vigilante, etc. He swings through the city, web to web, to stop crimes and help local New Yorkers feel safer to live in a place they all call home.

Spidey takes a selfie with a fellow New Yorker!

Spider-Man's Characteristics:

  1. Interacts with citizens and visitors alike all around New York
  2. Takes down the evil of the city from thugs to doctors with, uh... Robot arms
  3. Cleans up after his throw downs (His webs dissolve after a little while)

Spidey's Friends

As far as most of New York is considered, we're all Spider-Man's friends. He does have a few crime fighting allies and those on the side of the law though. Yuri Watanabe, police captain for NYPD, often helps direct local police officers, and Spider-Man, across the city to stop crime in its tracks. Other friends include: The Fantastic 4, more directly The Human Torch a.k.a Johnny Storm, Deadpool, Captain Marvel and Captain America, The Wolverine, and Iron Man a.k.a Tony Stark.
